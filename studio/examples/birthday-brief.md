# Game Brief — Sofia · 30th Birthday  (EXAMPLE)

## The moment
- **Occasion:** Sofia turns 30; surprise party on Saturday.
- **Reveal:** projected on the living-room TV at the party; she plays with a
  wireless keyboard while everyone watches.
- **Emotional goal:** she laughs the whole way and gets misty at the ending
  toast. "Turning 30 with these idiots is a gift."
- **Deadline:** Friday night.

## The recipient
- Name: **Sofia** ("Sofi")
- Look: short black hair, always in a mustard-yellow hoodie
- Personality: competitive, dramatic, secretly sentimental
- Gaming experience: casual (Mario Kart at parties) → forgiving difficulty
- Natural role: fast & sneaky glass cannon (she WILL brag about her damage)

## The cast
| Who | Relationship | In-game role | Notes |
|-----|-------------|--------------|-------|
| Marco | boyfriend | party healer | terrible cook, unshakeable optimist |
| Lea | best friend | party tank | gym rat, protects everyone |
| Gerald | her monstera plant | companion familiar | she talks to it daily |
| The Espresso Machine | office nemesis | mid-boss | broke on her first day, never forgiven |
| "Dirty Thirty" | the number 30 itself | final boss | defeated by acceptance, not damage |

## Raw material
1. She says "absolutely criminal" about minor inconveniences
2. The Ibiza trip where she lost a shoe in the sea (it washed back the next day)
3. She waters Gerald while telling it office gossip
4. Marco's risotto incident (smoke alarm, two pans lost)
5. Her sworn enemy: the office espresso machine "Brunhilde"
6. Karaoke song she always closes with: "Total Eclipse of the Heart"

## Tone boundaries
- Great to joke about: the shoe, Brunhilde, Marco's cooking, her drama
- Off-limits: her job hunt earlier this year
- Surprises to preserve: the final toast text (from Marco, verbatim below)

## Practicalities
- Languages: EN + IT (her family is Italian)
- Playtime: ~10 min
- Verbatim: final toast — "Thirty looks criminal on you. Absolutely criminal. — M."

## Assumptions
- Party of 3 heroes (Sofia, Marco, Lea) + Gerald as familiar — cast is small
  enough that everyone named appears.
- Levels: office (library theme, Brunhilde boss) → Ibiza beach (underwater
  act for the shoe) → party venue (final boss "Dirty Thirty", ends in toast).
