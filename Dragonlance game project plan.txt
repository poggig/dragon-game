Platformer Dragonlance Campaign - "Chronicles of Azurerune"
If you are Claude you can use this document to update with me the project plan. At the beginning this is a very simple concept idea but we will iterate. Keep it readable.

******* CONCEPT *******
A pixel-art platformer game covering Sessions 1-5 of the Dragonlance campaign set in the Azurerune Fortress universe.
- Story-driven with narration between and during levels
- Varied gameplay: combat, stealth, fishing minigame, boss fights, social/exploration
- Party system with character swap (4 heroes, each with unique abilities)
- Trilingual: English, Spanish, Italian
- HTML5 browser prototype first, then Godot 4.6 port

******* TECH STACK *******
Phase 1: HTML5/JavaScript/Canvas (playable in browser, easy to iterate)
Phase 2: Godot 4.6.1 port (Windows build, polished release)
GitHub: https://github.com/poggig/dragon-game

******* HEROES *******
1. KOTE - The Rogue/Champion
   - Role: Agility, precision, resourcefulness
   - Motivation: Rescue his missing mother
   - Abilities: Dash, ranged attack (bow/dagger throw), lockpick interaction
   - Visual: Dark-haired young man with dual swords (see Session 1 silhouette)
   - Sprite: classic_character.zip (closest match)
   - Key moments: Arena victory, fishing champion, shoots Spectator's eye, sees mother in ritual, dock battles

2. MINERVA DRAGONSOUL - The Warrior/Knight
   - Role: Strength, combat, protection
   - Motivation: Aspiring Knight of Solamnia, dragon seeker
   - Abilities: Heavy melee (greataxe), shield block, charge attack, rage (grows giant in L5)
   - Visual: Woman with red braided hair, dragon-wing motif, Solamnic armor (from L4)
   - Sprite: Super Pixel Heroes - Knight.zip (warrior variant)
   - Mentor choice: Debates between Becklin (sword) and Cudgel (tactics)
   - Key moments: Arena coordination, MVP vs Darkmantles, stops boulder, saves villagers, fights Ogre

3. ELBER - The Druid/Ranger
   - Role: Magic, nature, intelligence, technology
   - Motivation: Seeking technology of the floating cities
   - Abilities: Speak with animals, ranged magic, entangle, stealth bonus
   - Visual: Man with goggles, mechanical gauntlet, inventor aesthetic
   - Sprite: Super Pixel Heroes - Knight (1).zip (ranger variant)
   - Key moments: Arena teamwork, interrogates fauna, river jump escape, pins Goblin Chief

4. NESTA SAUDOTHIEN - The Dark Mage/Drow
   - Role: Dark magic, intimidation, arcane power
   - Motivation: Family vengeance against an unknown general
   - Abilities: Shadow bolt, fear aura, dark diplomacy, arcane assault
   - Visual: Hooded woman with bow, ranger-like (L1), dark elegance later
   - Sprite: Super Pixel Heroes - Knight (2).zip (dark variant)
   - Availability: Present L1, ABSENT L2-L3 (punished for scarring Bakaris), returns L4-L5
   - Key moments: Scars Bakaris (L1), fox familiar killed (L1), dark diplomacy for boats (L5), arcane assault (L5)
   - Familiar: Black fox (killed by Bakaris in L1 - emotional catalyst)

5. NICK OLEODON - Support NPC (not directly playable, provides buffs/healing)
   - Role: Necromancer/Cleric, divine support, healing
   - Motivation: Mission to liberate Balifor
   - Visual: Dark robed figure with skulls, necromantic aesthetic
   - Companion: Arturito (skeleton familiar, sacrifices himself in L5)
   - Appears in cutscenes and provides party buffs during combat

******* KEY NPCs *******
- LORD BAKARIS THE YOUNG: Main rival. Arrogant noble with team (Ayik Ur, Hrigg, Iriad, Andrella).
  Nesta scars his face in L1 after he kills her fox. His father gains power in Kalaman. Lifelong enemy.
- ISPIN GREENSHIELD: Legendary explorer, Nature professor. Invites party to Vogler (Kingfisher Festival).
- BECKLIN UTH VIHARIN: Fencing instructor, retired Solamnic Knight. Dies heroically in L5.
- CUDGEL IRONSMILE: Tactics & teamwork expert. Co-awards Solamnic armor in L4.
- LOHEZET: Enigmatic Arcane Master, Black Robe. Offers Nesta the dark pact in L4.
- LEVNA DRAKEHORN: Fellow student, Knight of the Rose. Has lightning scar from Blue Dragon.
- CLYSTRAN: Discusses dragon theories. His village Heart's Hollow may hold metallic dragon clues.
- DARRETT HIGHWATER: Wants to be Becklin's squire. Follows path of the sword.
- ANDIR VALMAKOS: Keeps faith in Old Gods (Paladine, Habbakuk) despite mockery.
- KANSALDI FIRE EYES: Red Dragon Army commander. Unbeatable final boss in L5.

******* LEVEL DESIGN *******

--- LEVEL 1: School Day at Azurerune ---
Session: 1 - "Día de Escuela: El Desafío de los Héroes en la Fortaleza Azurerune"
Setting: Azurerune Fortress (underwater academy under indestructible dome)
Available heroes: All 4 + Nick as NPC (Kote, Minerva, Elber, Nesta)
Gameplay type: Tutorial + Arena combat + Stealth/exploration + Story

Structure:
  Act 1 - THE FINAL EXAM (Tutorial + Arena combat)
    - Introduction: underwater fortress, marine life visible through crystal dome
    - The Tribunal watches: Ispin, Lohezet, Becklin, Cudgel
    - TUTORIAL: teaches movement, attacks, character swap
    - ARENA BATTLE: Heroes vs Bakaris team (Bakaris, Ayik Ur, Hrigg, Iriad, Andrella)
    - Team coordination wins over individual glory
    - Earn respect of Levna Drakehorn and Darrett Highwater
    - Environment: Pixel Crawler - Castle Environment (arena area)

  Act 2 - BLOOD AND GRUDGE (Cutscene + Quick combat)
    - Bakaris is a sore loser - kills Nesta's black fox familiar
    - Nesta retaliates: SCARS Bakaris's face permanently
    - Nick heals the physical wounds but the hatred remains
    - "A lifelong enemy has been forged"
    - This is the emotional core - establishes Nesta/Bakaris rivalry

  Act 3 - HALLWAY RUMORS (Exploration/social)
    - Explore Azurerune corridors between classes
    - Talk to NPCs: Levna (Blue Dragon scar story), Clystran (dragon theories, Heart's Hollow),
      Darrett (wants to be Becklin's squire), Andir (faith in Old Gods)
    - Bakaris drops political intel: his father gaining power in Kalaman, unknown invading force
    - Minerva's mentor choice: Becklin (sword) vs Cudgel (tactics) - dialogue choice
    - Environment: Castle Environment (corridors, classrooms)

  Act 4 - NIGHT INFILTRATION (Stealth)
    - Sneak into the Restricted Section of the Library at night
    - Obstacles: magical traps, surveillance mechanisms
    - MIMIC BOOKS attack! (Use Mimic Chest.zip - reskinned as books)
    - Key discovery: Azurerune's Secret Library has clues to the "Lost Library of Istar"
      in the Northern Wastes (Eriales del Norte)
    - Environment: Pixel Crawler - Library (dark, nighttime palette)

  Act 5 - DIVERGING PATHS (Story cutscene)
    - Students choose mentors and loyalties near graduation
    - Mages of High Sorcery path vs Path of the Sword
    - Ispin Greenshield invites the party to Vogler for the Kingfisher Festival
    - "Next stop: Vogler" - map shows route from Azurerune to the village
    - Sets up Level 2

Boss: Bakaris team (arena fight - tutorial boss, not too hard)
Key moments: Arena victory, fox familiar death, Nesta scars Bakaris, library infiltration
Key items: Intel about Library of Istar
Environment assets: Castle Environment (fortress, arena, corridors), Library (night infiltration)

--- LEVEL 2: The Kingfisher Festival ---
Session: 2 - "El Festival del Martin Pescador"
Setting: River journey to Vogler + Vogler fishing village
Available heroes: Kote, Minerva, Elber (Nesta absent - punished)

Structure:
  Act 1 - RIVER AMBUSH (Side-scrolling combat on boat)
    - Party travels by river to Vogler
    - 3 Darkmantles attack (squid-manta hybrids)
    - They cast magical DARKNESS (screen goes dark, limited visibility)
    - Minerva is MVP - melee focus
    - Environment: Pixel Crawler - Sewer or Cave (dark water theme)
    - Enemies: Use Bat_Fur.zip or alien monster sprites for Darkmantles

  Act 2 - FESTIVAL SABOTAGE (Exploration/social)
    - Arrive at Vogler village (Garden environment)
    - Talk to NPCs, meet Mayor Raven Uth Vogler
    - Elber talks to seagulls (reveals best fishing spots)
    - Elber talks to fish (reveals they love Salted Algae)
    - Buy 15kg of Salted Algae
    - Sabotage rival Aurel: get him drunk + steal his fishing rod
    - Sign up for Battle of High Hill reenactment

  Act 3 - THE FISHING COMPETITION (Minigame)
    - Fishing minigame: timing-based mechanic
    - Nick casts Blessing (buff), Minerva intimidates crowd, Elber handles bait
    - Must catch Benebog the Linebreaker (2.5m legendary fish)
    - Reward: 3 Pearls (Red, White, Black) from Benebog's belly

  Act 4 - SHADOW ON THE ROAD (Combat cliffhanger)
    - Returning from festival at dusk
    - Draconian Kapak ambushes from the bushes (glowing eyes, dagger)
    - Mini-boss fight, ends with acid explosion on death
    - Loot: Poisoned Kukri
    - Leads into Level 3

Boss: Draconian Kapak (acid death explosion)
Environment assets: Garden (village), Sewer/Cave (river), Free Pack (roads)

--- LEVEL 3: Library of Secrets ---
Session: 3 - "Infiltracion en la Biblioteca de Azurerune"
Setting: Azurerune Fortress Library (forbidden section)
Available heroes: Kote, Minerva, Elber (Nesta still absent)

Structure:
  Act 1 - THE SILENCE THRESHOLD (Stealth/puzzle)
    - Silence Zone: no verbal communication (visual-only hints)
    - Threat: Lord Ramses the familiar cat (170dB sonic meow = alarm)
    - Kote picks the lock
    - Move Professor Fazzir's candle without waking him or the cat
    - Environment: Pixel Crawler - Library

  Act 2 - DESCENT TO THE FORBIDDEN (Platformer with traps)
    - Transition from library shelves to damp underground corridors
    - Find the Candle of the Depths (burns underwater) in a fishbowl
    - Nick triggers pressure plate - caught in hanging net (must free him)
    - ROLLING BOULDER TRAP: Kote drinks Giant Strength Potion + Minerva = stop boulder
    - Environment: Pixel Crawler - Cave / Hideout

  Act 3 - ECHOES OF THE PAST (Combat)
    - Animated sword controlled by cursed gauntlet (Ispin Greenshield's first sword)
    - Minerva pins gauntlet, Elber & Kote dismantle
    - Reward: Espada +1 de Ispin (green magic glow)

  Act 4 - DECEPTIONS AND DEPTHS (Puzzle + Boss)
    - Mimic chest! (identify correctly to avoid combat) - Use Mimic Chest.zip asset!
    - Dark circular pool blocks the path
    - Use Candle of the Depths to find submerged hatch
    - BOSS: The Spectator (Beholder-kin) - floating eye monster
      * Confusion rays cause friendly fire (Elber hits Minerva!)
      * Out of melee range - must use ranged attacks
      * Kote's arrow to central eye = killing blow
    - Environment: Pixel Crawler - Cave

  Act 5 - THE ESCAPE (Stealth)
    - Books collected: Dragons, Horrors, Golden Vault Secrets, Bones
    - Artifacts: Mechanical Amulet of Istar, Ispin's Sword, Kukri, Depth Candle
    - Becklin guards the exit corridor
    - Convince him with flattery (dialogue choice) OR sneak past
    - Escape to dormitories

Boss: The Spectator (floating eye with confusion rays)
Key items: Candle of Depths, Espada +1 de Ispin, Amulet of Istar
Environment assets: Library, Cave, Hideout

--- LEVEL 4: The Yurthgreen Masquerade ---
Session: 4 - "El Baile de Yurthgreen"
Setting: Underwater ballroom of Azurerune Fortress
Available heroes: All 4 (NESTA RETURNS!)
Gameplay type: Social/exploration/story-heavy + ritual climax

Structure:
  Act 1 - THE BALLROOM (Exploration/social)
    - Stunning underwater ballroom: crystal ceiling, three moons visible
    - History floor made of sunken ship wood
    - Heroes in masquerade outfits (special sprites)
    - Nesta: dark elegance | Minerva: golden dragon | Elber: cape & mystery | Kote: noble simplicity
    - Talk to NPCs: Bakaris (rival, boasting), Levna (Knight of the Rose, tense)
    - Minerva & Clystran dance together
    - Tem Temble worried about Kendermore
    - Becklin & Cudgel dance (teachers having fun)

  Act 2 - THE LEGACY (Story/cutscene)
    - Minerva receives Solamnic armor from Becklin & Cudgel (gameplay upgrade!)
    - "The torch has been passed"

  Act 3 - THE SHADOW OF SORCERY (Story choice)
    - Lohezet approaches Nesta
    - Rite of the Golden Vault: ancient relics needed to defeat the enemy
    - The Promise: power & information in exchange for Black Robes initiation
    - Player choice: Accept? Refuse? (affects Nesta's abilities going forward)

  Act 4 - ISPIN'S RITUAL (Climax)
    - Ispin performs the ritual using the 3 pearls
    - Kote sees his mother briefly (emotional cutscene)
    - Three moons align through the crystal ceiling
    - Magic pulses - the student phase is officially over

  Act 5 - LIBRARY DISCOVERIES (Story)
    - The Golden Museum: contacting spirits is the key
    - Lost Gods: connection between relics and possible return of gods (good AND evil)
    - "The music fades, but the adventure has just begun"

Boss: None (story level - tension is social/emotional)
Key moments: Nesta's return, Solamnic armor, Kote sees his mother, moon alignment
Environment assets: Castle Environment (ballroom), custom underwater overlay

--- LEVEL 5: When the Home Burns (Fall of Vogler) ---
Session: 5 - "Cuando Arde el Hogar"
Setting: Vogler village - under siege
Available heroes: All 4
Gameplay type: Multi-phase battle, boss rush, escape sequence

Structure:
  Act 1 - CALM BEFORE THE STORM (Exploration/shop)
    - Vogler armory: 50% discount on weapons/armor
    - Festival of the Kingfisher at High Hill
    - Battle of High Hill reenactment begins...

  Act 2 - THE BROKEN ILLUSION (Twist + combat)
    - The "Iron Regiment" actors reveal real steel weapons
    - Red Dragon Army mercenaries in disguise!
    - Elber & Kote spot the trap first
    - Full combat begins on the hilltop
    - Minerva saves 2 villagers (carries them to safety)
    - Elber's desperate jump across the river
    - Environment: Garden (festival) → Desert (burning fields)

  Act 3 - LA RUINA ROJA (Escalation)
    - Kansaldi Fire Eyes' ultimatum echoes magically:
      "People of Vogler: By order of Kansaldi Fire Eyes, the wrath of Takhisis..."
    - Full-scale invasion, not a skirmish
    - SURVIVAL LOGISTICS: 400 villagers, boats for 200
    - Nesta threatens fishermen for extra boats (Dark Diplomacy)
    - Kote, Minerva & Nick convert Ferry Tavern into evacuation raft

  Act 4 - FIRST WAVE (Boss: Sivak Draconian)
    - A guard reveals true form: massive Sivak Draconian
    - Minerva enters rage (grows giant!)
    - Supported by Elber's arrows and Nick's magic
    - The beast falls, revealing the murdered guard's face

  Act 5 - IRON AND FIRE (Boss: Boilerdrak)
    - Mechanical fire-breathing dragon arrives at the docks
    - Flanked by Baaz and Bozak Draconians
    - Multi-phase boss: avoid fire breath, attack weak points
    - Becklin delivers the killing blow (NPC assist)
    - Nesta's arcane assault decimates draconian ranks
    - Nick provides covering fire from rooftops
    - Environment: Forge (burning docks) + fire overlay effects

  Act 6 - LAST STAND (Boss: Goblin Chief on Ogre)
    - Magical webs trap Nick and Nesta
    - Minerva vs Ogre (melee duel)
    - Elber pins Goblin Chief against wall
    - Nick heals wounded, Arturito (skeleton companion) sacrifices himself

  Act 7 - SIN ESPERANZA (Cutscene/Escape)
    - Kansaldi Fire Eyes arrives personally
    - Impossibly powerful - sinks a refugee boat with divine power
    - Kills Becklin with her pike ("Run, children!")
    - This is NOT a winnable fight - the heroes must FLEE
    - Escape sequence: run to the boats while dodging Kansaldi's attacks
    - Final shot: survivors float toward Kalaman, Vogler burns behind them

Final Boss: Kansaldi Fire Eyes (unwinnable - escape sequence)
Key items: Upgraded weapons from armory
Environment assets: Castle (village), Forge (burning docks), Desert (battlefield)
Effects: Super Pixel Effects Gigapack (fire, explosions), Explosion FX Pack
